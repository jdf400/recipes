## 1. High Protein Cajun Steak Mac & Cheese

**By @jalalsamfit** *A creamy, high-protein take on comfort food with a spicy kick.*

### **Macros (Per Serving)**

> **Calories:** 550 | **Protein:** 62g | **Carbs:** 42g | **Fat:** 14g
> *(Makes 4 servings)*

### **Ingredients**

**The Steak**

* **800g** Raw Topside (Top Round) beef, cubed
* **1.5 tsp** Salt
* **2 tsp each:** Black pepper, Garlic powder, Parsley
* **1 tsp** Chilli powder
* **2 tsp** Olive oil
* **40g** Light butter (for cooking)

**Creamy Cheese Sauce**

* **200g** Uncooked macaroni
* **280ml** Low-fat milk
* **150g** Light cream cheese
* **35g** Light cheddar
* **35g** Shredded mozzarella

**Cajun Seasoning Mix**

* **1 tsp each:** Salt, Pepper, Garlic powder, Onion powder, Paprika, Chilli powder (or Cayenne), Thyme, Oregano

---

### **Instructions**

1. **Boil Pasta:** Cook macaroni according to package instructions.
2. **Sear Steak:** Season cubed beef with steak seasonings. Heat a pan over **medium-high heat**. Add olive oil and butter, then sear beef for **3–4 minutes per side** to get a nice crust. Remove and set aside.
3. **Make Sauce:** In the same pan, add milk and light cream cheese. Whisk until smooth. Stir in the cheddar, mozzarella, and the **Cajun Seasoning Mix**.
4. **Combine:** Toss the cooked macaroni and seared steak into the sauce. Stir until every piece is coated and creamy.

## 2. Creamy Garlic Cheesy Chicken & Potatoes

**By @jalalsamfit** *A perfectly balanced one-pan style meal with crispy air-fried potatoes.*

### **Macros (Per Serving)**

> **Calories:** 502 | **Protein:** 53g | **Carbs:** 38g | **Fat:** 15g
> *(Makes 4 servings)*

### **Ingredients**

**The Chicken**

* **800g** Cubed chicken breast
* **3-4** Garlic cloves, minced
* **2 tsp each:** Onion powder, Mixed herbs, Paprika, Olive oil
* **1 tsp each:** Chilli flakes, Salt

**Crispy Potatoes**

* **800g** Potatoes, cubed
* **1 tsp each:** Salt, Garlic powder, Mixed herbs, Chilli flakes, Paprika
* **Optional:** Olive oil or cooking spray

**Garlic Parm Sauce**

* **120ml** Milk of choice
* **100g** Light cream cheese
* **40g** Grated parmesan
* **50g** Shredded mozzarella
* **Garnish:** Fresh parsley & extra seasoning

---

### **Instructions**

1. **Crisp the Potatoes:** Toss cubed potatoes in seasonings.
* **Air Fry:** 200°C (400°F) for 18–20 mins.
* **Oven:** 200°C (400°F) for 20–23 mins.


2. **Cook Chicken:** Season chicken and cook in a pan for **5–6 minutes** until browned.
3. **Simmer Sauce:** **Lower the heat** (crucial step!). Add milk and cream cheese to the chicken, stirring until creamy. Add parmesan and extra herbs.
4. **The Melt:** Stir in the crispy potatoes. Top with shredded mozzarella, cover the pan for **4–5 minutes** until the cheese is fully melted. Garnish with parsley.
